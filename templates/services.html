Blood Donation Connectivity?

Through this website we try to make sure that the blood reaches the person in need at the earliest. With a state wide database to store all available blood pouches we make sure the people in need have a one stop search through our website. They can find blood type and quantity to definitely meet their requirements. We have various demographic and statistic analysis which can be accessed by the admin and can be essential in future planning of medical facilities.

Role-wise provisons by our website


Roles:

Donor

Donors are the main contributors to our community who can select a desired hospital and date and time as per convenience for themselves and directly go to the hospital to donate blood for the noble cause.

Hospital

Hospitals serve as the connecting link from donors to the recipients. Hospitals not only can view the donation slot bookings made by various donors but also, they can see the various blood pouches. Hospitals can add pouches donated by donors through our website but also the ones which were not donated through a slot from our website. They can view the pouch bookings if made by recipients. Hospitals need not worry about the 42-day expiration of blood pouches as our website automatically deletes the pouches which were donated before 42 days.

Recipient

Recipients are the people whose work gets easier through our one-stop website. Recipients can see the various blood pouches and other necessary details of the blood pouches to match the needs of the patient and book a blood pouch. Also, they can view their bookings which shall be necessary to have hassle free pouch collection from hospitals. We on purpose have not kept an option of online payment because in such times of need the person in need might not be using online payment methods. But at the hospitals while collecting the blood pouch all payment methods shall be made available to suite the convenience.
