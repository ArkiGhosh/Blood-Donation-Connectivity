What do we do?

We, at Inspired Engine, build projects that make cumbersome tasks as hassle free as possible for a user. Our goal is to target any task that can be automated and build web apps that would turn an hour's errand into a 5 minute job. We believe that efficiency is the key to living a smoother life. A patient who needs blood urgently, need not run around the city and make calls anymore to check where they can find a blood pouch of a matching blood type. We built a portal with everything one place, in order to cater to such needs.

Our mission is to use this notion in order to move on to various fields so that we can provide the most user friendly experience.
